Louis had to work his way back from the bottom up at a small restaurant. He often threatened his co-workers, especially the head chef as he could not handle not being the best anymore. More and more serial killing cases of nurses and teachers have been occurring, it has been found that Louis is a common link between all these women as they have all been seen having dinner with him. Upon further investigation it was found that Louis was also behind the serial killing case of his girlfriend Miranda back on Earth, and the many before that. There is now a team out searching for him as he has fled.
