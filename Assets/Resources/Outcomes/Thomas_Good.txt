Thomas kept up his work ethic after moving to Evandria. He continues working hard to produce good music, and due to his efforts the music industry in Evandria became one of the largest industries in a short amount of time. As he got more famous, Thomas realized that he’s already proven his worth and starts to relax his schedules a little for his own benefit as well as his coworkers. He starts his own family and eventually has a son whom also wants to pursue the same career as his father.
