Sara stayed on Earth, along with her two children. After her unsuccessful attempt in applying for Evandria, she thought it was time to change her life around. She recently volunteered to help children with their education, but oftentimes she misses her meetings or arrives drunk. Rumours around the neighbourhood have said that she's on her way to lose her custody battle against her husband, and won't be able to see her children any longer. It may be a long while before Sara's able to get back up on her feet...
