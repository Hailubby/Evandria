On Evandria, Francis continues to develop his fsociety project. Having finally found the inspiration to pursue his passion, he spent months working on it, eventually finishing his grand design. He released the program to the public, all around Evandria people's personal debts were instantly wiped, one of the dominant banking institutions collapsed overnight. As thousands of citizens found happiness, so did Francis, as he knew he had finally done something for the good of the people. Following the sucsess of his project, Francis began to work for the government of Evandria helping develop a fair software system to handle the economy.
