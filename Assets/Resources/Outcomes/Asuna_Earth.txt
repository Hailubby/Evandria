Asuna continues to work hard at her current job, but it is barely enough to support both her and her sister's gargantuan medical treatment cost. She has had recurring thoughts about stealing again in order to procure enough funds to treat her sister's leukemia. She met up with her stepmother once again, but had an outburst of uncontrollable anger with her, largely in part still due to her experiences from her childhood, and her inability to afford psychiatric treatment sessions.
