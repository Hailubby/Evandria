Gabriel became much more devoted to his research on diseases affecting the human nervous system. He pioneered a treatment that helped prolong the life of the citizens of Evandria. Showing a change from his previous demeanour, he became more social by attending more work functions and getting to know his new peers better. However, there are times when Gabriel suffers from panic attacks, remembering the death of his family, showing he has a long way to go before he fully recovers from the trauma of that event.
